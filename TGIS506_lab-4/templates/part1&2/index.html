Number of Pokemon in each of the primary Pokemon types

Pokemon type is a property or as it was introduced during the first generation, an element, which defines the kinds of moves a character may have. Pokemon originally only had one type. However, this changed as the new generations were released for further expansion away from the classic "Fire", "Water", "Ice". Now, Pokemon can have one or two types. Notice there are no "Wind" or "Earth" as the prototypical elements referenced in fantasy are usually something like you would see in the 90s cartoon Captain Planet, i.e. Earth, Fire, Wind, Water, and Heart.
